Lasagne Recipe

The recipe for lasagne is a world classic, incapsulating the savoury flavours of mozarella cheese with the starchy contents of wheat

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Wheat
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps:

  1. Grab your favbourite tomato sauce
  2. Add the sauce into a pan and let it simmer in a pan
  3. Boil your noodles and get the cheeses ready
  4. Add a cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles, and more sauce
  5. Top up with a layer of cheese
  6. Reapeat steps 4-5 until you have three layers
  7. Bake until bubble and you're ready to dive in!
