Chipotle Chicken Recipe

The recipe for chiptole chciken is inspired by none other than...Chipotle!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Chili Peppers in Adobo Sauce
  • Various Spices

Steps:

  1. Prepare your sauce using a Ninja Blender
  2. Place 1 cup of the following: Cumin, Pepper, Salt, Chili Powder
  3. Drain 4 chili peppers and 6 ounces of adobo sauce
  4. Blend the sauce and let 6 chicken thighs marinate in it for 8 hours
  5. After marinating, cook on a iron skillet pan for 10 minutes and enjoy!
