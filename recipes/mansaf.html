Mansaf Recipe

The recipe for mansaf is a staple of Jordanian culture

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Lamb
  • Yogurt
  • Pita

Steps:

  1. Cook 4 cups of rice
  2. Prepare the lamb in the oven at 350 for 2 hours
  3. Prepare the yogurt by boiling it in a pot
  4. Prepare the pita at the bottom of the plate
  5. Place the rice on top of the pite and the lamb on top of the rice
  6. Pour yogurt over the dish and serve!
